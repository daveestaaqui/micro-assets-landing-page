Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

SporlyWorks ("we", "us", "our") develops the OmniSuite browser extensions and mobile applications designed to enhance web productivity. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use any of our products. Our core philosophy is simple: No bloat. No tracking. Pure productivity.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • License Keys: When you purchase an OmniSuite PRO upgrade, we store a license key locally in your browser to validate your purchase. This key is linked only to your Stripe transaction.
  • Email Address: If provided during checkout via Stripe, your email is used solely to deliver your license key and handle support requests.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect browsing history, keystrokes, or page content.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or fingerprinting.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.
  • We do not transmit any data from your browser to our servers during normal offline usage.

2.3 Network Requests

The only outbound network request our extensions make is a license validation check to our secure validation server when you enter a license key. This request sends only the key itself and receives a valid/invalid response. No other data is transmitted.

3. Local Storage

Our extensions (such as the Bookmark Manager, Code Snippet Manager, etc.) store your saved data entirely locally on your device using your browser's built-in chrome.storage or localStorage API. We have no access to this data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe: We use Stripe to process payments. When you purchase PRO, your payment data is handled securely by Stripe. We do not have access to your credit card details.
  • Google Play Store: For our Android applications, Google may collect crash logs or usage metrics according to your device's Android settings and the Google Play privacy policy.
