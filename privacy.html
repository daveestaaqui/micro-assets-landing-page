Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Overview

FlowKit ("we", "us", "our") develops browser extensions for Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and other Chromium-based browsers. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data We Collect

Extensions (Free & Premium)

We collect zero browsing data. All FlowKit extensions run 100% locally in your browser. We do not:

  • Track your browsing history
  • Read or store page content
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or telemetry inside extensions
  • Share data with third parties

The only data stored by extensions is your local preferences (e.g., toggle state), saved via chrome.storage.local and never transmitted.

Landing Page & Blog

Our website at daveestaaqui.github.io uses:

  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymous, aggregated traffic analytics (page views, referrers). No personal data is collected.
  • Formspree — if you voluntarily submit your email via the newsletter form, Formspree processes it. See Formspree Privacy Policy.

Premium Subscriptions

Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card number. Stripe provides us with:

  • Your email address (for license key delivery)
  • Subscription status (active, cancelled)
  • Transaction amounts

See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

3. License Key Validation

Premium extensions may validate your license key against our server. This request sends only the license key — no browsing data, no page content, no personal information.

4. Data Retention

License keys and associated emails are retained for the duration of your active subscription plus 30 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and equivalent regulations, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of email communications

To exercise these rights, email sandwichfitness@gmail.com.
