Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

TL;DR

✅ We collect ZERO personal data. All FlowKit extensions run 100% locally in your browser. We don't track you, we don't sell data, we don't use analytics.

1. Information We Collect

None. FlowKit extensions do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing data, or usage analytics.

  • • We do not collect names, emails, or contact information
  • • We do not track browsing history or page visits
  • • We do not use cookies, pixels, or tracking scripts
  • • We do not integrate with any analytics platforms
  • • We do not send any data to remote servers

2. Local Storage

Some extensions use Chrome's chrome.storage.local API to save your preferences (e.g., dark mode settings, saved snippets). This data:

  • • Is stored only on your device
  • • Is never transmitted anywhere
  • • Can be deleted by uninstalling the extension

3. Permissions

FlowKit extensions request only the minimum permissions needed to function:

  • • activeTab — Access the current page only when you click the extension
  • • storage — Save preferences locally on your device
  • • scripting — Inject analysis scripts on the current page

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party data services. Payment processing for premium extensions is handled by Stripe, which has its own privacy policy.

5. Children's Privacy

Our extensions are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact
