Free Tab Manager for Chrome — Save 80% RAM

Chrome is notorious for memory usage. With 20+ tabs open, Chrome can easily consume 4-8GB of RAM. Tab Suspender fixes this automatically.

How It Works

Tab Suspender monitors your inactive tabs and automatically suspends them after a configurable timeout (default: 30 minutes). When you click a suspended tab, it reloads instantly.
