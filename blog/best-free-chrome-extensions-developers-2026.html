Best Free Chrome Extensions for Developers in 2026

Finding the right developer tools can save hours of work every week. Here are the best free Chrome extensions that every developer should have installed in 2026.

1. JSON Formatter

Stop squinting at minified JSON. A good JSON formatter automatically detects and formats JSON responses with syntax highlighting, collapsible trees, and clickable URLs. Look for one that's under 50KB — most popular ones are bloated at 2-5MB.

Our pick: OmniSuite JSON Formatter — 5KB, zero tracking.

2. Color Picker / Eyedropper

Every frontend developer needs a color picker. The best ones let you pick colors from any webpage, show hex/rgb/hsl values, and copy with one click.

3. Tab Suspender

Developer work means dozens of tabs. A tab suspender can save 80% of your browser's RAM by suspending inactive tabs. This is especially important on 8GB or 16GB machines.

4. Screenshot Capture

Full-page screenshots for bug reports, design reviews, or documentation. The best tools don't require an account or upload your screenshots anywhere.

5. Dark Mode

Reduce eye strain during long coding sessions. A good dark mode extension works on ALL websites, not just popular ones.
