How to Reduce Chrome RAM Usage by 80%

Chrome is known for eating your computer's memory. With 20+ tabs open, Chrome can easily use 4-8GB of RAM. Here's how to fix it.

The Problem

Each Chrome tab runs in its own process, consuming 50-300MB of RAM. With 20 tabs, you could be using 4GB just for your browser.

The Solution: Tab Suspender

Install Tab Suspender. It automatically suspends inactive tabs, freeing their memory. When you click a suspended tab, it reloads instantly.

Real-World Results

  • 10 tabs: 2.1GB → 450MB (78% reduction)
  • 20 tabs: 4.2GB → 850MB (80% reduction)
  • 50 tabs: 9.1GB → 1.2GB (87% reduction)
