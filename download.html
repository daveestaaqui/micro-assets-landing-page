Install the Suite Natively.

Because your workflow shouldn't be governed by big-tech app stores. Pick the tools you want, extract them locally, and unleash full utility with zero telemetry.

Download the Installer

The Desktop GUI fetches the latest uncompressed extensions directly from our secure CDN and drops them right into your `SporlyWorks_Suite` folder.

Windows Mac OS Linux

How to Activate

  1. Run the setup application to download your selected extensions.
  2. Open your Chromium browser (Chrome, Edge, Brave).
  3. Go strictly to `chrome://extensions` in your address bar.
  4. Toggle Developer Mode on the top right.
  5. Click Load unpacked and select the folders inside `~/SporlyWorks_Suite/`.

Android Users

Standard Google Chrome on Android does not inherently support installing extensions. To experience the SporlyWorks Suite on mobile, we recommend installing the open-source Kiwi Browser from the Play Store. Once installed, simply download the extensions as ZIP files, open `chrome://extensions` within Kiwi, and sideload them directly!
