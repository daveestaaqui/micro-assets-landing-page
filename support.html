⌨️ Pro Tips

Pin extensions

Click the puzzle icon in Chrome → pin your favorite FlowKit extensions for one-click access.

Keyboard shortcuts

Go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts to assign custom hotkeys to any extension.

Works on Edge, Brave, Arc

All FlowKit extensions work on any Chromium browser — not just Google Chrome.

Sync across devices

Enable Chrome Sync to auto-install your extensions on every device you sign into.
